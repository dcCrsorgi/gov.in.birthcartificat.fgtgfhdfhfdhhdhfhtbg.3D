About Sabalpur

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Sabalpur village is 234553. Sabalpur village is located in Sonepur subdivision of Saran district in Bihar, India. It is situated 8km away from sub-district headquarter Sonepur (tehsildar office) and 46km away from district headquarter Chhapra. As per 2009 stats, Sabalpur Pachami is the gram panchayat of Sabalpur village.

The total geographical area of village is 261 hectares. Sabalpur has a total population of 27,262 peoples, out of which male population is 14,503 while female population is 12,759. This results in a sex ratio of approximately 879 females for every 1,000 males. Literacy rate of sabalpur village is 49.23% out of which 56.32% males and 41.16% females are literate. There are about 4,345 houses in sabalpur village. Pincode of sabalpur village locality is 841101.

Sonepur is nearest town to sabalpur village for all major economic activities, which is approximately 3km away.
